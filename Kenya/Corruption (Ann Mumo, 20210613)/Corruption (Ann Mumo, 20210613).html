Corruption

1) Governance: 
For our governments to be stable and effective, we must fight corruption. 

2) Strong Judicial System: 
We should ensure that leaders and government agents became answerable to the taxpayer.

3) Education: 
The public should be educated on the ills of corruption.

4) Right to Information (RTI) Against The Corrupt Citizens: 
While those who have stolen public funds are made to return it and face the full force of the law.

5) Right to Information (RTI) Against The Tax Havens:
Again, people known to have stashed money in foreign banks should be forced to repatriate that money so as to improve cash flow in our economies. This will put our countries on the road to prosperity.

6) Digitization of The Economy:
Through digitization of the economy, we can bring in transparency, and corruption would reduce.
